Things are not happy and peaceful anymore. Here's what happens: No one believes Harry: Harry says the evil Lord Voldemort is back, but the Ministry of Magic doesn't listen. They make Harry feel like a liar and try to shut him up. A new mean teacher: Dolores Umbridge is Hogwarts' new Defense Against the Dark Arts teacher. Overall, Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix is a great adventure story that shows how Harry is growing up and getting ready for even bigger challenges ahead.
