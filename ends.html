It Ends with Us


It Ends With Us: A Review This book tells the story of Lily, a strong woman who runs a successful flower shop. Her life takes a turn when she meets Ryle, a handsome neurosurgeon. Their connection is undeniable, but there's a catch: Ryle doesn't do relationships. Lily finds herself drawn to him anyway, but things get complicated. Memories of her troubled past with Atlas, her first love, keep resurfacing through letters she wrote back then. The story goes back and forth between Lily's present life with Ryle and her past with Atlas. We learn they shared a deep love, but something forced them apart. What you'll love: The characters are relatable. Lily is easy to root for, and both Atlas and Ryle are complex and interesting. The story is full of twists and turns that will keep you guessing. It tackles important themes about love, loss, and moving forward from a difficult past. Things to consider: The book deals with sensitive topics like domestic violence. If this is a trigger for you, it might be a good idea to choose a different book. The romance can be a bit intense at times, with a love triangle element. Overall: It Ends With Us is a powerful and emotional read that will stay with you long after you finish the last page. It's a story about love, second chances, and the courage to break free from unhealthy relationships.
