Harry Potter and The Prisoner of Azkabans


Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban: A Spooky School Year! Harry Potter is back for his third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry! But this year isn't all Quidditch and yummy treats. A dangerous escaped prisoner named Sirius Black is on the loose, and everyone says he's after Harry. Things get creepy with scary soul-sucking Dementors patrolling the school grounds, making Harry feel miserable. Harry also meets some cool new characters, like Professor Lupin, the new Defense Against the Dark Arts teacher, and a cheeky hippogriff named Buckbeak. He even learns a strange new trick that comes in super handy later. This book is full of suspense and mystery. Harry, Ron, and Hermione try to figure out who Sirius Black really is and why he escaped Azkaban. There are secret tunnels, hidden truths, and a thrilling time-travel adventure that will have you guessing until the very end. What makes it good? Exciting and suspenseful story that keeps you guessing Introduces great new characters like Professor Lupin and Buckbeak Shows Harry growing up and facing his fears Has a mix of scary moments, funny jokes, and heartwarming friendships This book is for you if: You like mysteries and adventures Want to learn more about Harry's past Enjoy a good dose of magic and wonder So, grab your copy of Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and get ready for a thrilling ride!
